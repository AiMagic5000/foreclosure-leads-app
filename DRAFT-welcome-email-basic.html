How Your Free Leads Work

Your program includes 75 verified leads delivered weekly at no additional cost. You can request your leads directly from your dashboard. All leads are screened against the National Federal Do Not Call Registry before delivery.

Every lead you receive comes skip traced with phone numbers, email addresses, property and mailing addresses, case numbers, APN data, and the recovery deadline for that state.

How the Commission Split Works

When you help a homeowner recover their surplus funds from a foreclosure sale, the standard contingency fee is 30% of the recovered amount. There are no upfront costs to the homeowner.

As a Asset Recovery Agent, that 30% fee is split 50/50 between you and Foreclosure Recovery Inc. For example, if you recover $20,000 in surplus funds, the 30% fee is $6,000 -- you keep $3,000.

Your Partnership Agreement: The commission structure, lead delivery terms, and program details are outlined in your Partnership Agreement contract. If you have not received your contract yet or have questions about any of the terms, reply to this email or call us at (888) 545-8007.
