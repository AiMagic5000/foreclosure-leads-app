Set Up Your Outreach Accounts

Your dashboard includes built-in ringless voicemail and SMS tools that work directly from each lead record. To use these features, you will need to set up the following accounts:

Ringless Voicemail

SlyBroadcast

To get your ringless voicemail automation up and running, we need you to create a SlyBroadcast account that we will use for your campaigns. SlyBroadcast allows you to drop personalized voicemails directly to your prospects without their phone ringing -- a proven way to generate warm callbacks.

Please follow these steps:

1.  Go to slybroadcast.com/signup.php and create a new account
2.  Add a payment method and deposit $10 to activate the account (this is required by SlyBroadcast to enable the service)
3.  Once your account is created, reply to this email with your username and password so we can access the account to set up your automation

Once we receive your login credentials, we will handle the technical setup and get your ringless voicemail campaigns configured and ready to go.

SMS Text Messaging

TextBee

TextBee lets you send SMS messages to prospects directly from your leads dashboard. It works through an Android phone, so your texts come from a real phone number instead of a random short code. You will need an Android device to complete setup.

Please follow these steps:

1.  Go to app.textbee.dev and create a free account
2.  Download the TextBee app APK onto your Android phone (the download link is on your TextBee dashboard)
3.  Open the app on your phone and sign in with the account you just created
4.  Go back to your TextBee dashboard at app.textbee.dev/dashboard and click Generate API Key
5.  Once your API key is generated, reply to this email with your API key and Device ID so we can connect it to your dashboard

Once we receive your API key and Device ID, we will integrate SMS messaging directly into your leads dashboard so you can text any prospect with one click.

Dashboard Integration: Once your SlyBroadcast and TextBee accounts are set up, let us know and we will integrate them directly into your leads dashboard. From there, you can send ringless voicemails, text messages, and pre-built emails all from each lead record without leaving the dashboard.
